# 🚀 Mini Crisp: Ultimate Demo Walkthrough

Use this script to showcase the full power of your AI-driven communication dashboard. This flow is designed to highlight the "Wow" factors (Voice, AI Intelligence, and Automation) right from the start.

## ✅ Pre-Demo Checklist
- Ensure the backend is running: `uvicorn main:app --reload`
- Ensure you are logged in at `http://localhost:8000/login`
- Have your microphone ready.

---

## 1. 🎙️ The "Alive" Interface (Voice Identity)
*Start by showing that the system listens and understands context.*

**You Say:**  
> "Hi, who are you?"

**Expected Result:**  
- The AI responds with its persona: *"Hi I am DISCAAT KID an AI assistant for SCPL."*
- **Why it impresses:** Establishes immediately that this isn't just a static dashboard; it has an identity and is voice-active.

---

## 2. ⚡ Instant Data Control (The Filtering Feature)
*Show off the recently locked "Text-to-UI" control. Bosses love seeing data appear on command.*

**You Say:**  
> "Show me all emails from today."  
> *(or "Show me messages from last week")*

**Expected Result:**  
- The **Visitor List** (sidebar) instantly refreshes to show only matching conversations.
- The AI confirms: *"I've updated the visitor list to show emails from [date]."*
- **Why it impresses:** Demonstrates control over data without clicking complex filter menus.

---

## 3. 🧠 Deep Intelligence (Summarization & PDFs)
*This is the killer feature. Turn raw messy chat logs into executive reports.*

**You Say:**  
> "What did Samiksha say today?"  
> *(or "Summarize conversation with Samiksha from Jan 1st to Jan 10th")*

**Expected Result:**  
- A concise **Text Summary** appears in the chat window, synthesizing the discussion.

**Then You Say:**  
> "Generate a detailed PDF report for her."

**Expected Result:**  
- The AI generates a **Downloadable PDF Link**.
- **Action:** Click the link. Show the PDF with **Charts/Graphs** (Sent vs Received, Keyword Analysis).
- **Why it impresses:** It turns operational noise into strategic insight in seconds.

---

## 4. ✍️ Zero-Touch Workflow (Smart Composing)
*Show how much time you save on typing.*

**You Say:**  
> "Draft an email to Samiksha regarding the Q1 roadmap."

**Expected Result:**  
- The **Global Email Composer** modal pops up automatically.
- **To:** Pre-filled with Samiksha's email (found via smart search).
- **Subject/Body:** AI auto-generates a professional draft based on your prompt.
- **Why it impresses:** It acts like a real secretary—you dictate, it types.

---

## 5. 🔍 Rapid Access (Search & Navigation)
*Demonstrate speed.*

**You Say:**  
> "Search for [Name]"  
> *(e.g. any other customer in your DB)*

**Expected Result:**  
- AI lists matches: "1. Name (email)..."
- **You Say:** "Open chat with the first one."
- The dashboard navigates specifically to that user's chat history.

---

## 🎯 Closing Statement
*"As you can see, this system aggregates Gmail and WhatsApp, allows me to control the UI with my voice, generates executive reports on the fly, and automates my drafting process. It's not just a dashboard; it's an active agent."*

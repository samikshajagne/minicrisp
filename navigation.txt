Here is a comprehensive map of the Mini Crisp Admin Dashboard UI. You can refer to these sections when specifying where you want the AI to act:

1. Header & Navigation (Top Bar)
Account Selector: Dropdown to switch between email/WhatsApp accounts.
Add Account Button: Opens the modal to connect new IMAP/WhatsApp credentials.
DISCAAT KID Button: Opens the floating AI assistant box.
Resync Button: Triggers a deep refresh of all message history.
WhatsApp Inbox: Navigates to the specialized WhatsApp view.
Logout: Ends your session.

2. Sidebar (Left Pane)
Conversation Search: Text entry to find people or topics.
Advanced Filters: Expanded panel for Date Range, Keywords, Source (Email vs Chat), and "Has Attachments".
Status Chips: Quick toggle filters for "All", "Unread", or "Read" messages.
Visitor List: The vertical feed of your active and past conversations.

3. Chat Area (Center/Right Pane)
Chat Header: Displays the name/avatar of the person you're chatting with.
In-Chat Search/Filters: Specific keyword search and date filtering relative to the current open chat.
Export Tool: Button to download the current conversation as a .txt file.
Messages Area: The scrollable bubble feed of the conversation.
Compose Button: The large button at the bottom used to trigger the text editor.

4. Email Composer (The Editor)
Email Fields: Specific inputs for Subject, CC, and BCC.
Rich Text Editor: The main writing area (Quill editor).
AI Assist (✨ button): Triggers the AI to draft or polish your text.
Attachment Bar: Area to see and manage uploaded files.
Send Email Button: The final action to dispatch the email.

5. AI Assistant Box (Floating Widget)
Voice Mic (🎤): For one-off voice commands.
Continuous Loop (🔁): For "Ok Discat" wake-word mode.
Chat Feed: History of your interactions with the assistant.

Global Email Composer (Pop-Up Modal)
This is the modal that appears when you want to start a new email thread (not just replying to an existing chat).

Sender Preview: Shows which account you are currently sending from.
Recipient Field: Where the destination email address (To:) is entered.
Subject Line: For the email topic.
Global AI Assist (✨ AI): A dedicated button within the modal to help you draft the entire email from a prompt.
Main Message Body: A large text area for writing the content.
Attachment Manager: Includes a "Attach Files" button and a preview area for uploaded documents/images.
Send/Cancel Buttons: Primary actions to dispatch the email or discard the draft.
